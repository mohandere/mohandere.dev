# Design Systems for Devs

A design system is a collection of reusable components, guided by clear standards, that can be assembled together
to build any number of applications. It also comprises of Principles, Style guide and Patterns.

It’s NOT just a component library, here are all the things you need to think about while building a design system:

- Design Principles
- Branding guidelines
- Style Guide
- Design tokens
- Patterns
- Components
- Voice and Tone
- Content Strategy
- Documentation
- Dev standards
- Processes
- etc

## Goal of Design System
- Short term goal: Consistency
- Long term goals: Quality and Productivity.


## Actors in Design system
- Business
- Design Team
- Developers
  - Creators
  - Consumers
